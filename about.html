About Justice AI

Our Objective

Justice AI aims to revolutionize legal document processing by providing automated, accurate summaries of court transcripts and legal documents. Our goal is to save legal professionals countless hours of manual review.

Technology

Currently built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, our platform demonstrates the frontend experience of our summarization tool. The future version will integrate with advanced AI APIs for actual document processing.

Future Scope

Planned features include AI-powered legal analysis, case law references, and integration with legal research databases. We're committed to continuous improvement of our summarization algorithms.
